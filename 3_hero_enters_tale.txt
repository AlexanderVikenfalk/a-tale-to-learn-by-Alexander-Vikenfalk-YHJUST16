The hero of this story is an ordinary man. He lives a comfortable lives without the reach of dangers and adventures. He's a tall, average built and likeable guy. But he is the hero of our story nonetheless. 
In his pockets he always make sure to bring with him his pen. It's a pointy pen and it will make a good weapon if needed. It's also useful in case you would like to write something, like for example a letter to someone.
Our hero has got a sidekick called Luigi. Luigi used to be Marios sidekick but got sick and tired of all the plumbing.
